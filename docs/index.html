How it works

Up and running in under 60 seconds

No signup. No data collection. Pure browser-side filtering that just works.

1

Install the Extension

Add Naukri Smart Filters to Chrome in one click from the Chrome Web Store. No account or signup required.

2

Configure Your Filters

Click the extension icon and set your salary range, blacklist companies, set experience. All settings are saved securely in Chrome sync storage.

3

Browse Naukri Normally

Head to naukri.com and search as usual. Your filters apply automatically in real time — no page reload needed.

4

Apply to What Matters

Only jobs that pass your criteria are shown. The badge in the corner shows exactly how many were hidden and how many remain.
